**VLDB 2027 (CCF-A), Under Review** — First Author

LAVM proposes a verified semantic reuse framework for materialized view recommendation in database systems. The method leverages semantic analysis to identify and recommend materialized views that can be safely reused across queries, improving query performance while maintaining correctness guarantees.
