**WISA 2026 (CCF-C), Under Review** — Second Author and Corresponding Author (First Author: Advisor Xiuze Dong)

We propose EAC-RAG, a lightweight, training-free framework that performs claim-level hallucination detection and local correction for RAG systems. EAC-RAG decomposes LLM responses into atomic claims, matches each claim to the most relevant evidence via hybrid retrieval, assesses claim credibility through multi-signal fusion (semantic similarity, NLI, lexical overlap), and applies targeted corrections. Experiments on RAGTruth benchmark achieve F1=0.657, outperforming all zero-shot LLM-based baselines.
