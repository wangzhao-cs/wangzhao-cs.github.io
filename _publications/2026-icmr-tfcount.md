**ICMR 2026 (CCF-B), Accepted** — Student Second Author

TF-Count proposes a training-free exemplar-based method for class-agnostic cell counting using DINOv2 features. The approach leverages pre-trained vision features to count cells without requiring task-specific training, enabling robust counting across diverse cell types and imaging conditions.
