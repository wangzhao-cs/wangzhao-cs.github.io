**IEEE BIBM 2025 (CCF-B), Accepted** — First Author

This paper proposes DiffFNN-Med, a task-adaptive fuzzy neural network framework for interpretable medical drug recommendation. The model leverages fuzzy logic to enhance the interpretability of drug recommendations while adapting to different medical tasks through a task-specific neural architecture. Experimental results demonstrate improved performance and clinical interpretability over baseline methods.
