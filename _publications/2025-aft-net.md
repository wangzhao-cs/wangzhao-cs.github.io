**PRCV 2025 (CCF-C), Accepted** — Student Second Author

This paper introduces AFT-Net, an adaptive representation learning framework for robust crowd counting and localization. The model incorporates adaptive feature transformation mechanisms to handle diverse crowd densities and scenes, achieving robust counting performance across challenging real-world conditions. Experiments on benchmark datasets validate the effectiveness of the proposed approach.
