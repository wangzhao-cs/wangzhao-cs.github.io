**BlockSys 2026 (CCF-C), Accepted** — Second Author and Corresponding Author (First Author: Advisor Xiuze Dong)

AdaVerify is an adaptive multi-level verification framework that dynamically selects among four verification levels—trust, multi-node redundancy, optimistic challenge, and zero-knowledge proof—based on a cost-optimal decision that incorporates stake value, provider-specific attack probabilities, and gas price. Implemented as Solidity smart contracts on Ethereum with EZKL for ZK proof generation. Results show that AdaVerify's cost-optimal strategy reduces simulated net cost by 69.9% compared to the fixed-ZK baseline while achieving 98.7% attack detection.
