**ESWA (中科院一区), Under Review** — Second Author and Corresponding Author (First Author: Advisor Xiuze Dong)

This paper proposes HARE-Rec, a history-aware routing framework for explainable cold-start recommendation. The method leverages auditable semantic-atom diagnostics to provide transparent reasoning for recommendations to new users or items. By dynamically routing through historical interaction patterns, HARE-Rec achieves competitive recommendation accuracy while maintaining full explainability for cold-start scenarios.
